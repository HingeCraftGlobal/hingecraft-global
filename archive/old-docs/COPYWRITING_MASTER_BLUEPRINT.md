## üé® Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Principles

**Voice:** Inspiring, Inclusive, Action-Oriented, Transparent, Empowering

**Tone:** 
- **Professional yet approachable** - We're serious about our mission but accessible to everyone
- **Hopeful and optimistic** - We believe in abundance and resilience
- **Clear and direct** - No jargon, no fluff, just meaningful communication
- **Community-focused** - We speak as "we" not "I", emphasizing collective action

### Voice Characteristics

1. **Inspiring:** Use active voice, powerful verbs, and forward-looking language
   - ‚úÖ "We're building a future where..."
   - ‚ùå "We hope to maybe someday..."

2. **Inclusive:** Use "we," "us," "our" to create belonging
   - ‚úÖ "Join our community of changemakers"
   - ‚ùå "Join the exclusive club"

3. **Action-Oriented:** Focus on what we're doing, not just what we believe
   - ‚úÖ "We're creating microfactories that..."
   - ‚ùå "We think microfactories might..."

4. **Transparent:** Be honest about challenges and progress
   - ‚úÖ "We're 30% complete and actively building"
   - ‚ùå "We're almost done" (when we're not)

5. **Empowering:** Give people agency and show impact
   - ‚úÖ "Your contribution directly funds..."
   - ‚ùå "Donations help us"

## üîò Micro-Copy (Buttons, Labels, CTAs)

### Button Copy Standards

#### Primary Actions (Green/Blue)
- **"Join the Movement"** - Main CTA for membership
- **"Support the Mission"** - Donation CTA
- **"Get Started"** - Onboarding CTA
- **"Learn More"** - Information CTA
- **"Sign the Charter"** - Charter commitment CTA

#### Secondary Actions (Gray/Outline)
- **"Explore Projects"** - Browse projects
- **"View Details"** - See more information
- **"Contact Us"** - Reach out
- **"Read More"** - Expand content

#### Destructive Actions (Red)
- **"Delete"** - Remove item
- **"Cancel"** - Cancel action
- **"Remove"** - Remove from list

### Form Labels

#### Standard Field Labels
- **"Full Name"** (not "Name")
- **"Email Address"** (not "Email")
- **"Phone Number"** (not "Phone")
- **"Organization"** (not "Company" or "Org")
- **"Job Title"** (not "Title" or "Position")
- **"Message"** (not "Comments" or "Notes")
- **"Amount"** (for donations, not "Donation Amount")

#### Placeholder Text Format
- Use examples that are helpful, not generic
- ‚úÖ "Enter your email address" ‚Üí "you@example.com"
- ‚úÖ "Enter donation amount" ‚Üí "$25.00"
- ‚ùå "Enter text here"
- ‚ùå "Type something"

### Status Labels

#### Project Status
- **"Idea"** - Initial concept stage
- **"Planning"** - Actively planning and designing
- **"In Progress"** - Currently being built
- **"Blocked"** - Waiting on dependencies
- **"Review"** - Ready for review/approval
- **"Done"** - Completed and operational

#### Task Status
- **"Todo"** - Not started
- **"Doing"** - In progress
- **"Blocked"** - Cannot proceed
- **"Done"** - Completed

#### Priority Levels
- **"Urgent"** - Critical, needs immediate attention
- **"High"** - Important, prioritize soon
- **"Medium"** - Standard priority
- **"Low"** - Can wait if needed

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Error Messages & Notifications

### Error Messages

#### Form Validation
- **"Please enter a valid email address"** (not "Invalid email")
- **"Donation amount must be at least $1.00"** (not "Amount too low")
- **"This field is required"** (not "Field cannot be empty")
- **"Please select a payment method"** (not "Payment method required")

#### System Errors
- **"We're experiencing technical difficulties. Please try again in a moment."** (not "Error 500")
- **"Unable to process your request. Please check your connection and try again."** (not "Request failed")
- **"This feature is temporarily unavailable. We're working to restore it."** (not "Feature disabled")

### Success Messages

- **"Thank you! Your donation has been processed successfully."**
- **"Your information has been saved."**
- **"Project updated successfully."**

## üìù Copywriting Guidelines Summary

### Do's ‚úÖ
- Use active voice and action-oriented language
- Be clear, direct, and jargon-free
- Focus on benefits and impact
- Use "we" and "our" for community feel
- Be transparent about status and progress
- Show, don't just tell

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't use corporate jargon or buzzwords
- Don't make false promises or overstate progress
- Don't use passive voice unnecessarily
- Don't be vague or ambiguous
- Don't use "I" when "we" is more appropriate
- Don't hide challenges or setbacks
