HINGECRAFT GLOBAL, LLC

COMMUNITY CODE OF CONDUCT

Effective Date: December 6, 2025
State of South Carolina

1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

1.1 Purpose. This Community Code of Conduct ("Code") establishes the standards of behavior expected of all participants in the HingeCraft community. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone.

1.2 Scope. This Code applies to all HingeCraft community spaces, including:

  • (a) HingeCraft website forums and discussion boards;
  • (b) Social media channels and groups;
  • (c) Email lists and newsletters;
  • (d) In-person events, trade shows, and meetups;
  • (e) Online events, webinars, and virtual gatherings;
  • (f) Customer support interactions;
  • (g) Any other space designated as a HingeCraft community space.

1.3 Participants. This Code applies to all community participants, including customers, partners, suppliers, employees, contractors, and any other individuals engaging with the HingeCraft community.

2. OUR VALUES

The HingeCraft community is built on the following values:

  • Respect: We treat everyone with dignity and respect.
  • Inclusion: We welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds.
  • Collaboration: We work together to solve problems and share knowledge.
  • Integrity: We act honestly and transparently.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and new ideas.
  • Professionalism: We maintain professional standards in our interactions.

3. EXPECTED BEHAVIOR

3.1 Be Respectful. Community members should:

  • (a) Treat all participants with courtesy and respect;
  • (b) Listen to and consider different viewpoints;
  • (c) Use welcoming and inclusive language;
  • (d) Respect others' time, opinions, and expertise;
  • (e) Give credit where credit is due;
  • (f) Accept constructive criticism gracefully.

3.2 Be Professional. Community members should:

  • (a) Keep discussions on-topic and relevant;
  • (b) Communicate clearly and constructively;
  • (c) Share accurate and helpful information;
  • (d) Respect intellectual property rights;
  • (e) Maintain confidentiality when appropriate;
  • (f) Represent HingeCraft products and services accurately.

3.3 Be Helpful. Community members should:

  • (a) Share knowledge and experience to help others;
  • (b) Answer questions thoughtfully and completely;
  • (c) Provide constructive feedback;
  • (d) Support newcomers and less experienced members;
  • (e) Contribute positively to discussions;
  • (f) Report issues and problems constructively.

3.4 Be Responsible. Community members should:

  • (a) Take responsibility for their words and actions;
  • (b) Correct mistakes promptly;
  • (c) Report violations of this Code;
  • (d) Comply with all applicable laws and regulations;
  • (e) Protect their own and others' personal information;
  • (f) Use community resources appropriately.

4. PROHIBITED BEHAVIOR

4.1 Harassment. The following behavior is prohibited:

  • (a) Offensive comments related to race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, disability, or physical appearance;
  • (b) Sexual harassment, including unwelcome sexual attention or advances;
  • (c) Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following;
  • (d) Threats of violence or incitement of violence;
  • (e) Publishing others' private information without permission ("doxxing");
  • (f) Continued one-on-one communication after being asked to stop;
  • (g) Any other conduct that creates a hostile environment.

4.2 Disruptive Behavior. The following behavior is prohibited:

  • (a) Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments;
  • (b) Personal or political attacks;
  • (c) Sustained disruption of discussions or events;
  • (d) Spam, off-topic content, or excessive self-promotion;
  • (e) Impersonating others or misrepresenting affiliation;
  • (f) Creating multiple accounts to evade bans;
  • (g) Interfering with community moderation.

4.3 Illegal and Unethical Conduct. The following behavior is prohibited:

  • (a) Sharing illegal content;
  • (b) Infringing intellectual property rights;
  • (c) Sharing malware or malicious links;
  • (d) Phishing or attempting to obtain credentials;
  • (e) Fraud or deceptive practices;
  • (f) Violating export control laws;
  • (g) Any other illegal activity.

4.4 Commercial Misuse. The following behavior is prohibited:

  • (a) Unsolicited advertising or promotion;
  • (b) Soliciting business from other community members inappropriately;
  • (c) Pyramid schemes or multi-level marketing;
  • (d) Selling counterfeit products;
  • (e) Misrepresenting products or services.

5. REPORTING VIOLATIONS

5.1 How to Report

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code, please report it:

  • (a) Email: community@hingecraft.com;
  • (b) Phone: (843) 555-0100;
  • (c) Use the "Report" function in community platforms;
  • (d) Contact any community moderator.

5.2 What to Include

When reporting, please include:

  • (a) Your contact information (optional for anonymous reports);
  • (b) Names or usernames of those involved;
  • (c) Description of the incident;
  • (d) Date, time, and location (online or physical);
  • (e) Screenshots or other evidence if available;
  • (f) Names of any witnesses.

5.3 Confidentiality

Reports will be handled confidentially to the extent possible. We will not disclose the identity of reporters without their consent, except as required by law.

5.4 No Retaliation

Retaliation against anyone who reports a violation in good faith is strictly prohibited and will be treated as a separate violation.

6. ENFORCEMENT

6.1 Investigation

Upon receiving a report, the community team will:

  • (a) Review the report promptly;
  • (b) Gather additional information as needed;
  • (c) Determine whether a violation occurred;
  • (d) Decide on appropriate action.

6.2 Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity of the violation, enforcement actions may include:

  • (a) Warning: A private warning to the individual;
  • (b) Content Removal: Removal of offending content;
  • (c) Temporary Suspension: Temporary ban from community spaces;
  • (d) Permanent Ban: Permanent removal from community;
  • (e) Account Termination: Termination of HingeCraft accounts;
  • (f) Legal Action: Referral to law enforcement for illegal activity.

6.3 Appeals

Individuals subject to enforcement action may appeal by emailing appeals@hingecraft.com within 14 days. Appeals will be reviewed by a different team member.

7. COMMUNITY MODERATION

7.1 Moderators. Community moderators are responsible for enforcing this Code. They have the authority to remove content, warn members, and escalate issues.

7.2 Moderator Conduct. Moderators are also bound by this Code and must act impartially and professionally.

8. SAFETY

8.1 Personal Safety. For in-person events, we are committed to providing a safe environment. If you feel unsafe, contact event staff immediately.

8.2 Online Safety. Protect yourself online by not sharing personal information publicly and using strong passwords.

8.3 Emergency. In case of imminent danger, contact local emergency services (911 in the U.S.) first, then notify HingeCraft.

9. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

By participating in the HingeCraft community, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by this Community Code of Conduct.
