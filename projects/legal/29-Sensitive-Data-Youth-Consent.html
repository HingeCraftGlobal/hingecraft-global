HINGECRAFT GLOBAL, LLC

SENSITIVE DATA & YOUTH CONSENT POLICY

Effective Date: December 6, 2025
State of South Carolina

1. PURPOSE

This Sensitive Data & Youth Consent Policy ("Policy") establishes HingeCraft Global, LLC's ("HingeCraft") standards for handling sensitive personal data and protecting the privacy of children and young people.

2. SENSITIVE DATA CATEGORIES

2.1 Definition

"Sensitive Personal Data" includes: (a) Racial or ethnic origin; (b) Political opinions; (c) Religious or philosophical beliefs; (d) Trade union membership; (e) Genetic data; (f) Biometric data; (g) Health data; (h) Sexual orientation; (i) Criminal history; (j) Financial account information; (k) Government ID numbers.

2.2 Collection Limitations

HingeCraft generally does NOT collect Sensitive Personal Data. When collection is necessary: (a) We obtain explicit consent; (b) We minimize data collected; (c) We implement enhanced security; (d) We limit access; (e) We retain only as long as necessary.

2.3 Lawful Basis

We process Sensitive Personal Data only when: (a) You provide explicit consent; (b) Processing is necessary for employment law; (c) Processing is necessary to protect vital interests; (d) Processing is required by law.

3. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA)

3.1 Age Restriction

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

3.2 Parental Consent

If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will: (a) Delete the information immediately; (b) Notify the parent/guardian if appropriate; (c) Take steps to prevent recurrence.

3.3 Parental Rights

Parents may: (a) Request access to their child's data; (b) Request deletion of their child's data; (c) Refuse further collection; (d) Contact us at privacy@hingecraft.com.

4. YOUTH DATA (13-17)

4.1 Enhanced Protections

For users ages 13-17: (a) We provide age-appropriate privacy notices; (b) We limit data collection; (c) We do not sell or share data for advertising; (d) We provide parental notification options.

4.2 Consent

Users ages 13-17 may provide their own consent for non-sensitive processing. For sensitive data, parental consent may be required under applicable law.

5. SECURITY MEASURES

Enhanced security for sensitive and youth data includes: (a) Encryption at rest and in transit; (b) Restricted access; (c) Audit logging; (d) Regular security assessments; (e) Incident response procedures.

6. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

Individuals (or parents on behalf of children) may: (a) Access their data; (b) Correct inaccuracies; (c) Request deletion; (d) Withdraw consent; (e) Object to processing; (f) Request data portability.
