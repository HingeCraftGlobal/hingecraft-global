Board Member Agreement

HINGECRAFT GLOBAL, INC.

Legal Duties, Liability, Confidentiality, and IP Protection

AGREEMENT

This Board Member Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between HINGECRAFT GLOBAL, INC. ("Company") and the undersigned Board Member ("Director") effective as of the date of execution.

I. FIDUCIARY DUTIES

1.1 Duty of Care

The Director agrees to exercise the care that a person in a like position would reasonably believe appropriate under similar circumstances, including:

  • Attending board meetings regularly
  • Reviewing materials in advance
  • Making informed decisions
  • Seeking expert advice when necessary

1.2 Duty of Loyalty

The Director agrees to act in good faith and in the best interests of the Company, including:

  • Avoiding conflicts of interest
  • Disclosing potential conflicts
  • Not competing with the Company
  • Not using Company opportunities for personal gain

1.3 Duty of Good Faith

The Director agrees to act honestly and in accordance with the Company's mission and values as set forth in the Corporate Charter and Stakeholder Ethos.

II. CONFIDENTIALITY

2.1 Confidential Information

The Director acknowledges access to confidential information including:

  • Business strategies and plans
  • Financial information
  • Proprietary technology
  • Customer and partner information
  • Employee information

2.2 Non-Disclosure

The Director agrees not to disclose confidential information except:

  • As required by law
  • With prior written consent
  • To other directors or officers in the course of duties
  • To professional advisors under confidentiality obligations

2.3 Duration

Confidentiality obligations continue indefinitely after termination of board service.

III. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

3.1 Company IP

All intellectual property created by the Director in connection with board service belongs to the Company.

3.2 Pre-Existing IP

The Director retains rights to pre-existing intellectual property, subject to Company's use rights as agreed.

IV. INDEMNIFICATION

The Company shall indemnify the Director to the fullest extent permitted by Delaware law for actions taken in good faith in the course of board service.

V. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement remains in effect during the Director's service on the Board and continues with respect to confidentiality and IP obligations after termination.

VI. GENERAL PROVISIONS

This Agreement is governed by Delaware law and may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.
