1. DEFINITIONS

"Buyers" means end customers who purchase Products through the Marketplace.

"Commission" means the fee charged by Marketplace Operator for each sale.

"Listing" means the product information displayed on the Marketplace.

"Marketplace" means the HingeCraft online marketplace platform.

"Products" means the goods offered by Merchant through the Marketplace.

2. MARKETPLACE SERVICES

2.1 Platform Access. Marketplace Operator grants Merchant a non-exclusive right to list and sell Products on the Marketplace, subject to this Agreement.

2.2 Services Provided. Marketplace Operator provides: (a) Product listing capabilities; (b) Order processing; (c) Payment processing; (d) Customer service support; (e) Reporting and analytics; (f) Marketing opportunities.

2.3 Merchant Obligations. Merchant shall: (a) Maintain accurate product information; (b) Fulfill orders promptly; (c) Provide customer support; (d) Comply with all policies; (e) Maintain required insurance.

3. PRODUCT LISTINGS

3.1 Listing Requirements. Listings must include: (a) Accurate product descriptions; (b) Clear product images; (c) Complete specifications; (d) Current pricing; (e) Accurate inventory levels; (f) Shipping information.

3.2 Prohibited Products. Merchant shall not list: (a) Counterfeit or infringing products; (b) Unsafe or recalled products; (c) Illegal products; (d) Products violating third-party rights; (e) Products not approved for the Marketplace.

3.3 Content Ownership. Merchant retains ownership of Listing content but grants Marketplace Operator a license to display, reproduce, and modify content for Marketplace purposes.

3.4 Listing Review. Marketplace Operator may review, approve, modify, or remove Listings at its discretion.

4.3 Payment Schedule. Payments are disbursed [weekly / bi-weekly / monthly] to Merchant's designated account.

4.4 Holds and Reserves. Marketplace Operator may hold funds for: (a) Returns and refunds; (b) Chargebacks; (c) Disputes; (d) Policy violations.

4.5 Taxes. Merchant is responsible for collecting and remitting applicable sales taxes. Marketplace Operator may collect taxes on Merchant's behalf where required by law.

5. ORDER FULFILLMENT

5.1 Fulfillment Requirements. Merchant shall: (a) Ship orders within [2] business days; (b) Use reliable shipping carriers; (c) Provide tracking information; (d) Meet delivery commitments.

5.2 Shipping Standards. Merchant shall: (a) Package products securely; (b) Include packing slips; (c) Use appropriate shipping methods; (d) Insure shipments as appropriate.

5.3 Performance Metrics. Merchant shall maintain: (a) On-time shipment rate: ≥95%; (b) Order defect rate: ≤1%; (c) Valid tracking rate: ≥95%; (d) Customer satisfaction: ≥4.0/5.0.

6. RETURNS AND REFUNDS

6.1 Return Policy. Merchant must accept returns for: (a) Defective products; (b) Products not as described; (c) Damaged products. Merchant may set additional return policies consistent with Marketplace standards.

6.2 Refunds. Refunds are processed through the Marketplace. Commission on refunded sales is not refunded except for Marketplace errors.

6.3 Disputes. Marketplace Operator may resolve disputes in its discretion, including issuing refunds from Merchant funds.

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 Merchant IP. Merchant retains all rights to its trademarks, products, and content.

7.2 Marketplace IP. Marketplace Operator retains all rights to the Marketplace platform, branding, and technology.

7.3 License. Merchant grants Marketplace Operator a license to use Merchant's name, logo, and product images for Marketplace purposes.

7.4 Infringement. Merchant warrants that Products and Listings do not infringe third-party IP. Merchant shall indemnify Marketplace Operator for IP infringement claims.

8. COMPLIANCE

8.1 Laws. Merchant shall comply with all applicable laws, including: (a) Consumer protection laws; (b) Product safety regulations; (c) Privacy laws; (d) Tax laws; (e) Export controls.

8.2 Marketplace Policies. Merchant shall comply with all Marketplace policies, as updated from time to time.

9. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other's confidential business information, including pricing, customer data, and business strategies.

10. INDEMNIFICATION

10.1 Merchant Indemnification. Merchant shall indemnify Marketplace Operator against claims arising from: (a) Merchant's Products; (b) Merchant's Listings; (c) Merchant's breach of this Agreement; (d) IP infringement.

10.2 Marketplace Indemnification. Marketplace Operator shall indemnify Merchant against claims arising from the Marketplace platform itself.

NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES. MARKETPLACE OPERATOR'S LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE COMMISSIONS PAID BY MERCHANT IN THE PRECEDING [12] MONTHS.

12. TERM AND TERMINATION

12.1 Term. This Agreement continues until terminated by either party.

12.2 Termination. Either party may terminate with [30] days' notice. Marketplace Operator may terminate immediately for policy violations.

12.3 Effect of Termination. Upon termination: (a) Listings are removed; (b) Pending orders must be fulfilled; (c) Outstanding payments are disbursed; (d) Confidentiality obligations survive.

13.1 Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by South Carolina law.

13.2 Disputes. Disputes shall be resolved by arbitration in Charleston, South Carolina.

13.4 Assignment. Neither party may assign without consent.
