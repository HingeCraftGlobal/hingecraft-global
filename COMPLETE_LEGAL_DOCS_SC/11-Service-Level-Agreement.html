1. INTRODUCTION

1.1 Purpose. This SLA defines the service levels, performance metrics, and remedies applicable to services provided by HingeCraft to Customer under the underlying services agreement (the "Services Agreement").

1.2 Scope. This SLA applies to all services covered by the Services Agreement, including but not limited to digital platform services, e-commerce services, customer support services, and professional services as specified.

1.3 Incorporation. This SLA is incorporated into and forms part of the Services Agreement. In the event of any conflict between this SLA and the Services Agreement, the Services Agreement shall control, except with respect to service level commitments and remedies, where this SLA shall control.

2. DEFINITIONS

"Availability" means the percentage of time the Services are operational and accessible, calculated as: ((Total Minutes - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes) × 100.

"Business Hours" means Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time, excluding U.S. federal holidays.

"Downtime" means any period during which the Services are unavailable or materially impaired, excluding Scheduled Maintenance and Exclusions.

"Incident" means any unplanned interruption or degradation of Service quality.

"Priority Level" means the classification of an Incident based on severity and impact.

"Resolution Time" means the time from Incident acknowledgment to restoration of Service.

"Response Time" means the time from Incident report to first substantive response from Provider.

"Scheduled Maintenance" means planned maintenance of which Customer is given at least 72 hours' prior notice.

"Service Credit" means a credit applied to Customer's account for failure to meet service levels.

3.2 Availability Measurement

Availability is measured monthly and calculated as follows:

  • (a) Measurement begins at the start of each calendar month;
  • (b) Total minutes in the month are calculated;
  • (c) Downtime minutes are subtracted from total minutes;
  • (d) Availability percentage is calculated using the formula above;
  • (e) Measurements are based on HingeCraft's monitoring systems.

3.3 Maintenance Windows

  • (a) Scheduled Maintenance: Sundays, 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM Eastern Time;
  • (b) Emergency Maintenance: As required for critical security or stability issues;
  • (c) Scheduled Maintenance is not counted as Downtime;
  • (d) Notice of Scheduled Maintenance will be provided at least 72 hours in advance;
  • (e) HingeCraft will use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize maintenance impact.

6. SERVICE CREDITS

6.1 Credit Calculation

If HingeCraft fails to meet the Availability commitment, Customer is entitled to Service Credits as follows:

Monthly AvailabilityService Credit (% of Monthly Fee)
< Target but ≥ 99.0%10%
< 99.0% but ≥ 95.0%25%
< 95.0% but ≥ 90.0%50%
< 90.0%100%

6.2 Credit Request

  • (a) Customer must request Service Credits within 30 days of the end of the affected month;
  • (b) Requests must include the dates and times of claimed Downtime;
  • (c) Requests should be submitted to sla@hingecraft.com;
  • (d) HingeCraft will verify claims within 15 business days;
  • (e) Approved credits will be applied to the next invoice.

6.3 Credit Limitations

  • (a) Maximum credit per month: 100% of monthly service fees;
  • (b) Credits are not refundable for cash;
  • (c) Credits do not carry over beyond 12 months;
  • (d) Credits are Customer's sole and exclusive remedy for Downtime.

7. EXCLUSIONS

7.1 Exclusions. The following are not counted as Downtime and do not qualify for Service Credits:

  • (a) Scheduled Maintenance during designated windows;
  • (b) Emergency maintenance for critical security or stability issues;
  • (c) Issues caused by Customer's actions, equipment, or third-party services;
  • (d) Issues outside HingeCraft's reasonable control (force majeure);
  • (e) Suspension of Services due to Customer's breach;
  • (f) Beta or preview features;
  • (g) Issues with Customer's internet connectivity;
  • (h) DDoS attacks or other malicious activity;
  • (i) Issues caused by Customer's failure to follow recommendations.

8. CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES

Customer is responsible for:

  • (a) Maintaining adequate internet connectivity;
  • (b) Using supported browsers and devices;
  • (c) Keeping contact information current;
  • (d) Providing accurate information when reporting Incidents;
  • (e) Cooperating with HingeCraft in troubleshooting;
  • (f) Implementing recommended configurations and updates;
  • (g) Maintaining security of Customer credentials;
  • (h) Timely response to HingeCraft requests for information.

9. REPORTING

9.1 Monthly Reports. HingeCraft will provide monthly reports including:

  • (a) Availability statistics;
  • (b) Incident summary;
  • (c) Response and resolution time performance;
  • (d) Any Service Credits earned.

9.2 Quarterly Reviews. Upon request, HingeCraft will conduct quarterly service reviews with Enterprise customers.
