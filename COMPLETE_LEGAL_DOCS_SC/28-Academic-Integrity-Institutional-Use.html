Effective Date: December 6, 2025
State of South Carolina

1. PURPOSE

This Academic Integrity & Institutional Use Policy ("Policy") establishes terms for the use of HingeCraft Global, LLC ("HingeCraft") products, services, and resources by educational institutions, researchers, and students.

2. EDUCATIONAL LICENSING

2.1 Eligibility

Educational licenses are available to: (a) Accredited K-12 schools; (b) Accredited colleges and universities; (c) Vocational and technical schools; (d) Non-profit research institutions; (e) Individual students and educators.

2.2 Educational Discounts

Eligible institutions may receive: (a) Discounted product pricing; (b) Free or reduced-cost software licenses; (c) Access to educational resources; (d) Sample products for curriculum development.

2.3 Application

Apply for educational programs at education@hingecraft.com with: (a) Proof of institutional affiliation; (b) Description of intended use; (c) Contact information.

3. RESEARCH USE

3.1 Research Licenses

Academic researchers may use HingeCraft products for: (a) Non-commercial research; (b) Published academic studies; (c) Educational demonstrations; (d) Student projects.

3.2 Publication Rights

Researchers may publish findings about HingeCraft products with: (a) Proper attribution; (b) Acknowledgment of HingeCraft; (c) Notification of significant publications; (d) Sharing of published papers.

3.3 Collaboration

HingeCraft welcomes research collaborations. Contact research@hingecraft.com for: (a) Joint research projects; (b) Data sharing agreements; (c) Equipment loans; (d) Technical support.

4. STUDENT USE

4.1 Student Projects

Students may use HingeCraft products for: (a) Class assignments; (b) Capstone projects; (c) Thesis and dissertation research; (d) Competitions (with attribution).

4.2 Academic Integrity

Students must: (a) Cite HingeCraft resources appropriately; (b) Comply with institutional academic policies; (c) Not present HingeCraft designs as original work; (d) Obtain permission for commercial use.

5. INSTITUTIONAL LICENSING

5.1 Site Licenses

Institutions may obtain site licenses for: (a) Campus-wide software access; (b) Lab equipment use; (c) Curriculum integration.

5.2 Terms

Institutional licenses specify: (a) Number of users; (b) Permitted locations; (c) Duration; (d) Renewal terms; (e) Support services.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Research conducted using HingeCraft products: (a) IP developed by researchers belongs to researchers/institutions; (b) Improvements to HingeCraft products may be jointly owned; (c) Specific terms may be negotiated in research agreements.

7. RESTRICTIONS

Educational licenses may NOT be used for: (a) Commercial purposes; (b) Consulting services; (c) Products for sale; (d) Non-educational research.
