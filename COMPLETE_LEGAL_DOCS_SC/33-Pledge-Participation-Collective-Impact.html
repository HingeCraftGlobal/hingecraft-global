COLLECTIVE IMPACT AGREEMENT

Effective Date: December 6, 2025
State of South Carolina

PREAMBLE

HingeCraft Global, LLC ("HingeCraft" or the "Company") recognizes that meaningful, lasting change requires collective action. This Pledge of Participation & Collective Impact Agreement (the "Pledge") establishes our commitment to working collaboratively with stakeholders to address societal challenges and create positive impact.

ARTICLE I - OUR PLEDGE

We, HingeCraft Global, LLC, pledge to:

  • Act as responsible corporate citizens in all communities where we operate;
  • Prioritize long-term value creation over short-term gains;
  • Treat all stakeholders with fairness, dignity, and respect;
  • Operate with transparency and accountability;
  • Minimize our environmental footprint and contribute to sustainability;
  • Support the well-being of our employees and their families;
  • Contribute to the economic vitality of our communities;
  • Collaborate with others to address shared challenges;
  • Continuously improve our positive impact.

ARTICLE II - COLLECTIVE IMPACT PRINCIPLES

2.1 Common Agenda

We commit to: (a) Engaging with stakeholders to identify shared priorities; (b) Aligning our efforts with community needs; (c) Participating in collective goal-setting; (d) Supporting initiatives that address root causes.

2.2 Shared Measurement

We commit to: (a) Establishing common metrics for impact; (b) Tracking and reporting on our progress; (c) Participating in collective assessments; (d) Using data to improve outcomes.

2.3 Mutually Reinforcing Activities

We commit to: (a) Coordinating activities with partners; (b) Leveraging our unique strengths; (c) Avoiding duplication of efforts; (d) Supporting complementary initiatives.

2.4 Continuous Communication

We commit to: (a) Regular dialogue with stakeholders; (b) Sharing learnings and best practices; (c) Building trust through open communication; (d) Listening and responding to feedback.

2.5 Backbone Support

We commit to: (a) Contributing resources to collective efforts; (b) Participating in coordination mechanisms; (c) Supporting shared infrastructure; (d) Maintaining long-term engagement.

ARTICLE III - FOCUS AREAS

3.1 Economic Opportunity

We support economic opportunity through: (a) Job creation and fair employment; (b) Local purchasing and supplier development; (c) Skills training and workforce development; (d) Support for entrepreneurship; (e) Financial inclusion initiatives.

3.2 Education

We support education through: (a) STEM education programs; (b) Scholarships and educational grants; (c) Partnerships with educational institutions; (d) Employee mentoring programs; (e) Technical training opportunities.

3.3 Environmental Sustainability

We support sustainability through: (a) Carbon reduction initiatives; (b) Waste reduction and recycling; (c) Water conservation; (d) Sustainable product design; (e) Environmental education.

3.4 Community Well-being

We support community well-being through: (a) Charitable contributions; (b) Employee volunteerism; (c) Support for local organizations; (d) Disaster relief efforts; (e) Community development projects.

ARTICLE IV - COMMITMENTS

4.1 Financial Commitment

HingeCraft commits to contributing [____]% of annual profits to community initiatives, distributed among our focus areas.

4.2 Time Commitment

HingeCraft provides [____] hours of paid volunteer time per employee per year for community service.

4.3 Partnership Commitment

HingeCraft commits to maintaining active partnerships with at least [____] community organizations.

4.4 Reporting Commitment

HingeCraft commits to annual public reporting on our collective impact activities and outcomes.

ARTICLE V - ACCOUNTABILITY

5.1 Oversight. The Board of Managers oversees implementation of this Pledge.

5.2 Reporting. Annual impact reports are published and shared with stakeholders.

5.3 Stakeholder Input. We regularly solicit stakeholder feedback on our impact efforts.

5.4 Continuous Improvement. We use feedback and data to continuously improve our approach.

ARTICLE VI - INVITATION TO PARTICIPATE

We invite our employees, customers, suppliers, partners, and community members to join us in this commitment. Together, we can create meaningful, lasting change.

Stakeholders may participate by: (a) Volunteering with partner organizations; (b) Contributing to community initiatives; (c) Sharing ideas and feedback; (d) Collaborating on impact projects; (e) Spreading awareness of collective efforts.

EXECUTION

This Pledge has been adopted by HingeCraft Global, LLC and is effective as of the date first written above.
