  • (b) Harm to Others: Harass, abuse, threaten, defame, or discriminate against any person or entity;

SECTION 3 - PRODUCT SAFETY

3.1 General Safety Requirements

Users of HingeCraft products must:

  • (a) Read and understand all product documentation, including installation instructions, user manuals, and safety warnings;
  • (b) Use products only for their intended purpose and within specified operating parameters;
  • (c) Ensure proper installation by qualified personnel when required;
  • (d) Perform regular maintenance and inspections as recommended;
  • (e) Use appropriate personal protective equipment when handling or working with products;
  • (f) Keep products away from children unless specifically designed for child use;
  • (g) Discontinue use immediately if any defect or malfunction is detected;
  • (h) Report any safety incidents, injuries, or near-misses to HingeCraft.

3.2 Installation Safety

When installing HingeCraft products:

  • (a) Follow all installation instructions precisely;
  • (b) Ensure the installation environment is suitable and meets specified requirements;
  • (c) Use only approved fasteners, hardware, and mounting systems;
  • (d) Verify structural integrity of mounting surfaces;
  • (e) Ensure proper weight distribution and load capacity;
  • (f) Allow adequate clearance for movement and operation;
  • (g) Test operation before placing the product into service;
  • (h) Document the installation for future reference.

3.3 Operational Safety

During operation of HingeCraft products:

  • (a) Do not exceed rated load capacities or operating limits;
  • (b) Ensure moving parts are clear of obstructions;
  • (c) Keep fingers, hands, and loose clothing away from pinch points;
  • (d) Do not operate damaged or malfunctioning products;
  • (e) Use appropriate lockout/tagout procedures during maintenance;
  • (f) Do not disable or bypass safety features or guards;
  • (g) Ensure adequate lighting and visibility;
  • (h) Train all operators on proper use and safety procedures.

3.4 Maintenance Safety

When maintaining HingeCraft products:

  • (a) Follow recommended maintenance schedules and procedures;
  • (b) Use only genuine HingeCraft replacement parts or approved equivalents;
  • (c) De-energize and lock out equipment before maintenance;
  • (d) Use appropriate tools and equipment;
  • (e) Keep maintenance records;
  • (f) Dispose of worn or replaced parts properly;
  • (g) Test operation after maintenance before returning to service.

3.5 Warning Labels

Users must:

  • (a) Not remove, deface, or alter any warning labels or safety markings;
  • (b) Replace any damaged or illegible warning labels;
  • (c) Heed all warnings and comply with all safety instructions.

  • (g) Contain malicious code or links to harmful websites;
  • (h) Violate any applicable law or regulation.

4.3 Technical Restrictions

Users may not:
